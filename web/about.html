About Us


We believe that everyone of you deserves to pursue toward mental wellness and healing with the help of excellent psychotherapy-support that is dependable and secure, thorough enough to address issues, and potent enough to make a difference. Because of this, EGIDE Therapy has redefined online therapeutic help by offering professional services hosted by carefully chosen psychologists and psychiatrists.

EGIDE Therapy is a sophisticated, fact-based, and research-based online platform created to assist you in maintaining your mental wellness. By creating reliable methods for data encryption and storage, we have assured security and safety while placing a high priority on a user-friendly framework. In order to assist you in overcoming any mental difficulties you or your loved ones may be experiencing, we have assembled a network of carefully chosen Arabic-speaking psychiatrists and psychotherapists as well as a referral network of specialist clinics and hospitals.

Our advanced Matching Tool can match you with a group of appropriate therapists that specialise in the issues raised by the questionnaire as soon as you begin using the EGIDE Therapy platform. The platform efficiently guides you down the path of successfully treating your problems through approaches based on empirical research that meaningfully and effectively effectuate improvement toward your mental wellness.

With the help of Alba Therapy, you may take the first step toward living a better, more well-rounded life.
